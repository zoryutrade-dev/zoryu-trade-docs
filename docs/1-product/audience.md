# Who Zoryu Is Built For

Zoryu Trade is designed for users who require more than surface-level trading tools.

## Target Users

- active Solana traders
- technically inclined users
- traders who value execution clarity and risk framing
- builders and analysts evaluating strategies

## Not Designed For

- guaranteed profit seekers
- passive signal consumers
- custodial trading workflows
- users unwilling to engage with execution mechanics

Zoryu prioritizes depth and reliability over mass-market appeal.
